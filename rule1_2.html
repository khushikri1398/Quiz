Rules and Guidelines

Gameplay for part 2

1. Question Selection:

  • A set of questions will be displayed on the screen, categorized by different topics, and each topic consists of 6 questions. Each question has a point value, and the higher the points, the tougher the challenges..
  • Participants will choose a topic and then select a question based on its point value.

2. Answering Questions:

  • The team answering the question correctly will earn the points assigned to that question and will have the opportunity to select and answer the next question.
  • If the team answers incorrectly, they will incur a negative marking as specified.
  • The question will be visible on the screen for 45 seconds.
  • Chits will be distributed to determine who starts the game and selects a question. A team member from each team will come up to pick a chit, which will contain either the number 0 or 1. If they draw the number 1, they will have the opportunity to select the question.

Scoring

Each correctly answered question will add the specified points to the team's total score. Incorrect answers will result in points being deducted as per the negative marking scheme.

Strategy

  • Choose questions wisely based on your team's confidence in the topic.
  • Manage your risk by balancing potential points with the possibility of negative marking.
