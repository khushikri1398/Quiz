Rules and Guidelines

Welcome to Round 2 of NexTech: The Ultimate Electronics Challenge. This round will test your practical skills and creativity in circuit tinkering.

Gameplay

1.Riddle Selection:

  • Some chits will be provided and in each chit a riddle is written.
  • Each team has to pick one chit and decipher the riddle.

2. Circuit Construction:

  • Once the riddle is deciphered, teams will have to design and construct a functional circuit that meets the riddle's criteria.
  • Teams will have 45 minutes to complete their circuit design and construction.

Evaluation Criteria:

  • Functionality: The circuit must perform the intended task as inferred from the riddle.
  • Innovation: Creative and efficient use of components and design techniques.
  • Neatness: Clean and organized construction of the circuit.
  • Documentation: Clear and thorough explanation of the design process and functioning of the circuit.
CRITERIA POINTS
Functionality 20
Innovation 15
Neatness 10
Documentation 05
